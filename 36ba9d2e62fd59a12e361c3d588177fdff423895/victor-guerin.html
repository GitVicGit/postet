Reversed Contretemps (2022)

h.100 cm l.100 cm

Digital

'Contretemps' is an oil painting that intentionally plays on the different meanings of this word in French and English.

French for 'mishap', 'Contretemps' offers an opportunity to meditate, take the time. As 'argument' (meaning in English) it reflects on the nature of our modern lives where calm and peace seem an anomaly.

This piece, 'Reversed Contretemps', is the prolongation of my painting 'Contretemps' that I created from a photography of the original painting and modified on software.

Oil on canvas

A celebration of the extensive ability of Nature to survive, its resilience, its ability to create, sustain and propagate life in a variety of environments.

It seems precious - does it matter?

Faux-Pas (2022)

h.79 cm l.60 cm w.4 cm

Oil on canvas

The pendent of 'Jouvence', but with an emphasis on the detrimental impact we human beings have on our planet. I found fascinating to see how Nature repopulated Chernobyl, against all odds, so there is also this slight message of hope in 'Faux-Pas': the footprint is getting covered.
